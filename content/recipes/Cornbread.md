# Cornbread

> This gluten-free cornbread recipe is simple, uses minimal ingredients, and
> serves as a fantastic side to soups, stews, and BBQ.

## Ingredients

- *1 cup* gluten-free flour blend
- *1 cup* gluten-free corn meal
- *0.25 cup* granulated sugar
- *2 tsp* gluten-free baking powder
- *0.5 tsp* salt
- *2* eggs (room temperature)
- *1 cup* milk
- *0.25 cup* oil of choice (or melted and cooled butter)

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Position rack in center of oven. Grease a 12 serving
cupcake pan; or a square 9x9x2-inch baking pan; set aside.

2. In a medium mixing bowl combine gluten-free flour blend, gluten-free corn
meal, granulated sugar, gluten-free baking powder, and salt.

3. In a separate medium mixing bowl, beat together eggs, milk, and oil of
choice. Add dry mixture to wet mixture and stir just till blended.

4. Spoon batter evenly into the square pan or cupcake pans, filling about
2/3 full. For a square pan bake for 25-30 minutes or until a wooden toothpick
inserted near center comes out clean. For muffins, bake for 20 minutes or until
a wooden toothpick inserted near center comes out clean.

5. Store leftover muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to
two days.
