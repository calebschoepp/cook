# Crockpot Cheesy Potatoes

> Crockpot Cheesy Potatoes are the easiest, tastiest slow cooker meal! This
> recipe is hearty enough to be a main dish and requires no prep work!

## Ingredients

- *32 ounces* potato
- *10.5 ounces* cream of chicken soup
- *0.75 cup* sour cream
- *2 tablespoons* butter (cubed)
- *1* onion (diced)
- *1 tsp* garlic salt (garlic powder)
- *1 tsp* onion powder
- *0.5 tsp* pepper
- *2 cups* cheese (shredded and divided)
- *1 pack* bacon (cooked and chopped)

## Cookware

- *4-6 quart * slow cooker

## Steps

1. Spray a slow cooker with cooking spray.

2. Pour the frozen potato in the bottom, top with cream of chicken soup, sour
cream, butter, onion, garlic salt, onion powder, pepper, and half of the cheese.

3. Cook on low for 6 hours, stirring at least once. Or cook on high for 3 hours,
stirring at least once.

4. Just before serving, stir in half of the bacon. Top the potatoes with the
remaining cheese and bacon. Place the lid on the potatoes for 5 minutes to melt
the cheese.
