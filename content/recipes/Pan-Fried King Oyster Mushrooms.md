# Pan-Fried King Oyster Mushrooms

> A super-savory pan-fried mushroom side dish made with King Oyster mushrooms
> (also called King Trumpet mushrooms).

## Ingredients

- *8-12 ounces* King Oyster mushrooms
- *2 tablespoons* olive oil
- *0.25 teaspoon* kosher salt
- *0.25 teaspoon* black pepper
- *2 tablespoons* unsalted butter
- *2 cloves* garlic (thinly sliced)
- *0.25 teaspoon* ground nutmeg
- *0.5 cup* parsley leaves
- *1* lemon (zested)
- kosher salt
- black pepper
- *1* lemon (zest)

## Cookware

- cast-iron skillet

## Steps

1. Preheat a large cast-iron skillet over medium-high for 5 minutes.

2. Slice King Oyster mushrooms in half lengthwise, leave smaller mushrooms
whole.

3. Reduce heat to medium and add olive oil; swirl to coat pan. Arrange mushrooms
in an even layer, as best you can, cut side down. Cook, without moving, for
3 minutes.

4. Season mushrooms with kosher salt and black pepper; toss and continue cooking
until golden brown all over, about 5 minutes.

5. Reduce heat to medium-low, add unsalted butter, garlic, and ground nutmeg and
cook for 2-3 minutes until garlic is toasted.

6. Remove skillet from heat and add parsley leaves and lemon.

7. Stir to combine then season with additional kosher salt, black pepper, and
lemon to taste.
