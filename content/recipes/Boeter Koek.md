# Boeter Koek

> Ryan's family recipe for Boeter Koek.

## Ingredients

- *1 cup* margarine becel
- *1 1/2 cups* sugar
- *2* eggs
- *1 1/2 tsp* almond extract
- *3 cups* gluten-free all-purpose flour

## Steps

1. Mix margarine becel and sugar thoroughly.

2. Add eggs one at a time, reserving 1/2 yolk in a small dish with a few drops
of water.

3. Mix in almond extract and gluten-free all-purpose flour until combined.

4. Press the dough into pan.

5. Spread the reserved yolk mixture evenly on top.

6. Bake at 450Â°F for 8-10 minutes.
