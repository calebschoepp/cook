# Chili Rice

> A simple one-pot beef, tomato, and rice dinner simmered with celery, onion,
> green pepper, and chili powder.

## Ingredients

- *1 lb* ground beef
- *0.5 cup* celery (chopped)
- *0.25 cup* onion (chopped)
- *0.25 cup* green pepper (chopped)
- *1 clove* garlic (minced)
- *2 tsp* salt
- *3 cups* water
- *16 oz* diced tomatoes
- *1 cup* long-grain rice (uncooked)
- *6 oz* tomato paste
- *1 tsp* sugar
- *1 tsp* chili powder
- *1* bay leaf
- bay leaf

## Cookware

- saucepan

## Steps

1. Brown ground beef in a large saucepan over medium heat. Drain off any excess
fat.

2. Add celery, onion, green pepper, garlic, and salt. Cook until the vegetables
are crisp-tender.

3. Stir in water, diced tomatoes, long-grain rice, tomato paste, sugar, chili
powder, and bay leaf.

4. Cover and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the rice is
cooked.

5. Remove the bay leaf before serving.
